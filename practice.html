Secretary Profile:-

Mrs. Monika Bhonwal was born in Lucknow. She had her education in lucknow.after doing Law graduation from Lucknow University she continued her study further and did her MBA & Post graduation Diploma in Human relation with Distinction. At a very early stage she started playing Badminton which in the course of time became her passion. She played various national & International tournaments. Some of her achievements in the field of badminton may be enumerated as follows.

>- Gold medal in National school Games in 1982, 1983 & 1984.
>- Gold medal in All India universities games in 1985.
>- Gold medal in All India women Nationals games 1985.
>- Gold medal in world Masters Games 2002 held in Australia.
>- Silver medal in the UAE open tournament 2008.

Mrs. Bhonwal is a multi talented woman. After playing Badminton she tried her hands in umpiring & became certificate umpire of Asian Badminton Confederation in 2005.apart from umpiring, she also organized State and National level badmintons tournaments and received accolades for her organizing capacity. This was not the full stop she continued her journey of achievements. She dared peep into the world of glamour and came with the flying colors winning the all India Reality shows- Lift Karade. After several round of audition she was chosen the biggest fan of Bollywood Star Kareena Kapoor. The event was hosted by Sony TV and anchored by famous film director Karan Johar.another milestone come in her life when she represented and won the title of charity ambassador and the queen of beautiful hair 2015. The event was organized in Delhi. Academic, sports & glamour is the one side of coin of her life and other side shows her emotional bondage with the down trodden. She has taken a adult education activities in the rural areas. She always stands for the traditional values of the Indian society and believe in the saying “where there is will there is way”
